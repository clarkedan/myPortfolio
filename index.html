Wonder Boy

This stop-motion animation of a late 80's video game was made by re-creating (pixel by pixel) the set of the first level of "Wonder Boy". I then took photos which portrayed the movement of the character, placed them in order using Adobe Premiere and laid the soundtrack on top of the animation.
